Posttraumatic stress disorder evaluation study
-- 

Clinical psychiatrists have conducted a study on the prevalence
of PTSD on a sample of current and previous military personel.
The scale of PTSD ranges from 0 to 1 in terms of severity. 

The data contains the variables:

    gender: man or woman
    alcohol: weekly average alcohol consumption
    smoking: regular smoking
    partner: single or not
    kids: number of kids
    age: age in years
    missions: number of missions 
    salary: current salary in DKK
    ptsd: PTSD score
    
    
Objective
--
We are interested in predicting who will be affected by PTSD
from the observed variables and which variables that are 
significantly affecting the outcome.
